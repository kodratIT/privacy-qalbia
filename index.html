1. Location Data

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Disclosure: The Qalbia application accesses and collects your location data to provide core application features including accurate prayer times and Qibla direction.

1.1 What Location Data We Collect

  • GPS Coordinates: Latitude and longitude of your device
  • Geographic Information: City and country names (obtained through reverse geocoding)
  • Timestamp: Time when location data is accessed
  • Accuracy Level: Measurement accuracy of the location

1.2 How We Use Location Data

We use your location data exclusively for the following purposes:

  1. Prayer Times Calculation: Calculate accurate prayer times based on your geographic location using the Aladhan API
  2. Qibla Direction: Determine the direction of the Kaaba from your current location
  3. Distance to Kaaba: Calculate the distance from your location to the Kaaba in Makkah
  4. Prayer Notifications: Send prayer reminder notifications based on your local timezone

1.3 Location Permissions Required

The application requests the following permissions:

ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION High accuracy location for precise Qibla direction calculation
ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION Approximate location for general prayer times estimation
ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION Location access when the app is running in the background for prayer schedule updates
‚úÖ Location Data Protection:
  • Location data is stored ONLY on your device (local storage/cache)
  • Coordinates are sent to Aladhan API (aladhan.com) solely for prayer times calculation
  • Aladhan API does not permanently store or track your location data
  • We do NOT store location history on our servers
  • Location data is NEVER sold, transferred, or shared with advertisers
  • We do NOT use location data for marketing or advertising purposes
